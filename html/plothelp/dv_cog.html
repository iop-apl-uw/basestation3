Course Over Ground Plot Help

This plot shows the glider's dead-reckoned track for the dive, plotted in latitude/longitude.

  • Course Over Ground is the dead-reckoned position track computed from the CTD time base (latitude/longitude netCDF variables). Hovering a point shows time into the dive, depth, and (when available) pitch/roll at that point.
  • GPS2 is the GPS fix taken at the start of the surface maneuver before diving.
  • GPSEND is the GPS fix taken at the end of the dive, on the next surfacing.
  • Arrows (when present) mark NEWHEAD guidance-computer messages - each shows the new commanded heading, with depth and time into the dive in the hovertext.
