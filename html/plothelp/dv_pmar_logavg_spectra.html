PMAR Log-Average Spectra Plot Help

This plot shows the PMAR channel's log-averaged power spectra over the course of the dive, for a single channel (named in the title).

  • Each CF <freq> Hz trace is the power (counts²/Hz, log-scale left axis) at one center frequency of the log-averaged spectrum, plotted against time into the dive (bottom x-axis) - the top x-axis shows the same points against GMT time.
  • The right axis shows depth, for correlating spectral power with where in the water column the glider was.

The x-axis subtitle reports summary profile statistics for the segment shown. Use the log-average plot to look for depth- or time-correlated changes in ambient noise across frequency bands, as opposed to the raw per-sample statistics on the channel statistics plot.
