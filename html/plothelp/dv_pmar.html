PMAR Channel Statistics Plot Help

This is a family of diagnostic plots for a single PMAR (passive acoustic) channel, each showing one summary statistic vs. depth, separately for dive and climb. The channel and statistic are named in the plot title and output file name.

  • Signal StdDev - standard deviation of the raw signal.
  • Signal Mean Abs - mean absolute value of the raw signal.
  • Clip Count (or Clip Count Normalized, when normalization data is available) - how often the channel's ADC clipped (saturated).

Colored shaded bands mark guidance-computer (GC) moves - pump, pitch, and roll motor actuations - so acoustic noise or clipping caused by the vehicle's own mechanisms can be distinguished from ambient/biological signal. The x-axis subtitle reports summary statistics (min/max/mean) for the dive or climb segment shown.
