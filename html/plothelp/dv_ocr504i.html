OCR504i Plot Help

This plot shows raw output from a Satlantic OCR504i radiometer vs. depth, dive and climb shown separately.

  • The three single-wavelength channels (412.3 nm, 443.58 nm, 554.47 nm) are on the bottom x-axis, in uW/cm²/nm.
  • The PAR (photosynthetically active radiation) channel is on the top x-axis, in umol/m²/sec.

The buttons below the plot toggle both x-axes between linear and log scale - light intensity typically falls off log-linearly with depth, so log scale is often easier to read throughout the water column.

Shaded/overlay regions (when present) mark sample timeouts and the commanded sampling range for the instrument - see the sampling traces help.
