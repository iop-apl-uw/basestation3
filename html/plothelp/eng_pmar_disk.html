PMAR Disk Usage Plot Help

This is the PMAR-specific version of the disk usage plot, tracking free space on the PMAR instrument's own storage media over the mission, vs. dive number.

  • PM <NN> free kB traces show free space per individual PMAR drive.
  • PM Total free kB (when more than one drive is present) is the sum across all drives.

The x-axis subtitle projects, from the recent free-space trend, how many dives/days remain before PMAR storage fills up and an estimated end date.
