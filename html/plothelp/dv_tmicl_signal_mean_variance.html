TMICL Signal Mean/Variance Plot Help

This plot shows raw-signal statistics vs. depth for one TMICL microstructure/shear-probe channel (ch0, ch1, shear, temp, temp0, or temp1 - named in the title), dive and climb shown separately.

  • Signal Mean (bottom axis, linear) is the channel's raw mean level.
  • Signal Variance and Integrated Variance (top axis, log scale, Volts/sec²) characterize the fluctuating (turbulent) part of the signal - elevated variance vs. depth is the signature of a turbulent layer.

For shear channels, the Signal Mean trace is hidden by default (toggle via the legend) since mean shear level is usually less diagnostic than its variance.

See also the log-average spectrum plot for the frequency content of these same channels.
