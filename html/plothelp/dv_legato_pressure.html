Legato Pressure Plot Help

This plot compares the raw RBR Legato pressure signal against the basestation's smoothed/despiked version and the truck pressure sensor, to diagnose pressure spikes and despiking behavior.

  • legato pressure is the raw pressure as reported by the Legato.
  • legato pressure spikes marks points the despiker flagged as bad (QC_INTERPOLATED) and interpolated over.
  • legato dp/dt (right axis, hidden by default) is the rate of pressure change, used by the despiker to detect spikes.
  • ctd_pressure is the basestation's smoothed/corrected pressure used for the rest of processing.
  • truck_pressure is the seaglider's own (non-Legato) pressure sensor, for cross-checking.
  • auxB_pressure/auxCompass_pressure (when present) is the pressure channel from an auxiliary compass, another independent cross-check.
