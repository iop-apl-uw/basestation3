Legato Plot Help

This plot displays Legato CTD data, as reported by the instrument - no data corrections or quality control passes have been applied.

All science traces on this plot report the data values, depth, time into the dive (in minutes) and the index of the data value in the dive in the hovertips. For scicon based instruments, the data index is for the combined profiles (dive, loiter, climb, surface loiter).



Legato Raw Salinity

The Legato Raw Salinity traces are derived from the eng_rbr_temp and eng_rbr_conduc netCDF varibles, if the legato is attached to the truck, or the legato_temp. and legato_conduc netCDF variable, if the legato is attched to the scicon, along with the pressure netCDF variable.



Legato Raw Temperature

The Legato Raw Temp traces are taken from the eng_rbr_temp netCDF variable, if the legato is attached to the truck, or legato_temp, if the legato is attached to the scicon.



Legato Raw Conductivity

The Legato Raw Conductvity traces are taken from the eng_rbr_conduc netCDF variable, if the legato is attached to the truck, or legato_conduc, if the legato is attached to the scicon. This trace is not tied to a x-axis - use the hovertips to interpret the values.



Legato Raw Conduc Temp

The Legato Raw Conduc Temp traces are taken from the eng_rbr_conducTemp netCDF variable, if the legato is attached to the truck, or legato_conducTemp, if the legato is attached to the scicon.
