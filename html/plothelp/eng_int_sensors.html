Internal Sensors Plot Help

This plot tracks the pressure hull's internal environment over the mission, vs. dive number - the basic vehicle-health check for leaks or desiccant exhaustion.

  • Internal Pressure (left axis, psia) - a rising trend over the mission can indicate a slow leak.
  • Relative Humidity (right axis) - a rising trend can indicate desiccant exhaustion or a leak.
  • Temperature (far-right axis, C) - internal temperature, for context when interpreting the other two.

Additional traces may appear here if a logger/sensor extension registers its own internal-sensor plot.
