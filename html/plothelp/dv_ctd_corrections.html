CTD Thermal-Inertia Corrections Plot Help

This plot compares the raw CTD data as reported by the instrument (Seabird or RBR Legato, shown in the title) against the basestation's thermal-inertia-corrected data, all plotted against time into the dive.

  • Salinity traces (left axis, PSU) and Temperature traces (right axis, C) are each shown in both their raw and corrected forms so the effect of the correction is visible directly.
  • A Depth trace (secondary left axis) gives context for where in the dive/climb profile a given correction is largest - thermal-inertia error is most visible where temperature/conductivity are changing quickly with depth.
  • The buttons above the legend toggle which trace group is shown.

The title includes a QC totals summary, comparing the count of each QC flag between the raw and corrected temperature/salinity data.
