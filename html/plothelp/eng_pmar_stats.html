PMAR Stats Plot Help

This plot tracks PMAR instrument health/operational counters over the mission, vs. dive number, summed across profile segments and reported per channel (ch00, ch01).

  • bufferfull/writeerrors (left axis) - counts of buffer overflow and write-error events.
  • num blocks (second left axis) - data blocks recorded.
  • clip count/despike count (right axis) - how often the channel's ADC clipped or a despike correction was applied.
  • num files (far-right axis) - files written.

A rising trend in bufferfull, writeerrors, or clip count over the mission points to a PMAR hardware or storage problem worth investigating before it gets worse.
