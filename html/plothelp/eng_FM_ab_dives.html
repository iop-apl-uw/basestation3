FM a/b History Plot Help

This plot is a FlightModel mission-summary plot, not a per-dive science plot. It shows the history of the hydrodynamic lift (a) and drag (b) coefficients FlightModel has committed to, dive by dive, over the course of a mission. a is scaled by 10 so it shares a sensible range with b on the same axis.



a*10

The lift coefficient FlightModel has committed to for that dive (flight_data.hd_a), scaled by 10. Lift is never considered independently "trusted" or "untrusted" the way drag is (see below), so it's always shown in the same color.



b / Trusted b

The drag coefficient FlightModel has committed to for that dive (flight_data.hd_b). Colored differently depending on whether the dive's grid-search solution is considered "trusted" - i.e. whether a direct velocity comparison instrument was available, or the dive/climb pitch difference was large enough to well-constrain the drag estimate (see trusted_drag()).



Grid solution markers (diamonds, with error bars)

On dives where FlightModel actually performed a fresh a/b grid search (not every dive triggers one - some dives reuse a previously-committed solution), a diamond marker with error bars shows the raw grid solution and the span of a/b values still within tolerance of the minimum misfit, for both a and b.
