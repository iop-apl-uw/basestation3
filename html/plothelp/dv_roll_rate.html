Roll Control vs Turn Rate Plot Help

This is an alternate way to derive the roll trim center (C_ROLL_DIVE/C_ROLL_CLIMB), using observed heading turn rate instead of raw roll angle - turn rate is less sensitive to compass noise than the roll angle itself. Dive and climb are shown separately.

  • Observed turn rate - dive/climb is turn rate (deg/sec) vs. roll control counts for all points in the segment.
  • Observed turn rate while centered restricts to points near the currently commanded roll center, where the relationship is closest to linear.
  • Current center marks the roll control value implied by the currently commanded roll center.
  • All data linear model and Centered data linear model are linear fits to, respectively, all points and just the near-center points, each reporting its own implied roll center.

Compare against the roll control plot, which derives the same parameter directly from roll angle rather than turn rate.
