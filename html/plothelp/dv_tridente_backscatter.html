Tridente Backscatter Plot Help

This plot shows all of the Tridente's optical backscatter channels (one wavelength per channel, e.g. bb700) together vs. depth, dive and climb traces shown separately (units: m-1sr-1 ×10-1).

See the Tridente fluorescence plot for the instrument's fluorescence channels (chlorophyll, CDOM, etc), which are plotted one channel per figure rather than combined.
