Pitch and Roll Motor Diagnostics Plot Help

This plot tracks pitch and roll motor performance across the mission, vs. dive number - one point per motor move.

  • current traces (left axis, A) show motor current draw per move.
  • rate traces (right axis, AD/s) show motor speed per move.

Rising current or falling rate over the mission for either motor can indicate increasing mechanical friction/drag, worth checking against the mission energy plot for a corresponding rise in modeled motor energy use.
