Volmax Estimates Plot Help

This plot compares several independent estimates of the vehicle's hull volume (volmax, cc) over the mission, vs. dive number. volmax is a core Flight Model parameter - the vehicle's neutrally-buoyant displaced volume - and it can drift over a long deployment (e.g. from hull compression/creep or biofouling).

  • Basestation Volmax is the basestation's per-dive estimate.
  • Basestation Regressed Volmax is a regression fit across recent dives rather than a single-dive estimate.
  • GSM Volmax is the steady-state (Gliderscope) model's estimate.
  • Glider On Board Volmax is the value computed onboard the vehicle itself.
  • Flight Model Volmax is the value currently in use by the Flight Model System.

Persistent disagreement between estimates, or a trending value, is worth investigating before it degrades vertical velocity/buoyancy predictions - see the vertical velocity plot.
