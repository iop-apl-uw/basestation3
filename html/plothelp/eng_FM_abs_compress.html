FM Compressibility Plot Help

This plot is a FlightModel mission-summary plot, not a per-dive science plot. It shows the vehicle's estimated absolute compressibility (abs_compress, units 1/dbar) as computed per dive over the course of a mission, and the mean value FlightModel has adopted from the deeper, more reliable dives.



Raw abs_compress

The per-dive abs_compress estimate, as solved by FlightModel for that dive.



Deep abs_compress

The subset of dives whose bottom pressure exceeded the ac_min_press threshold - deep dives give a more reliable compressibility estimate, so these are marked separately from the full "Raw abs_compress" series.



Mean (deep) abs_compress

The running mean of the deep dives' abs_compress values - this is the value FlightModel actually adopts as the mission's compressibility constant.
