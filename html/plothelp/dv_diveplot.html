Dive Plot Help

This is the primary engineering "everything" plot for a dive - most flight-control and vehicle-state channels overlaid vs. time into the dive, on a shared depth-scaled left axis (many traces are scaled/offset so they share the depth axis; the scale factor is given in each trace's legend label). Use the legend to toggle traces on/off.

  • Depth traces (truck, and Legato/aux CTD when present).
  • Vert Speed/Horiz Speed/Glide Angle - both flight-model (GSM/HDM) estimates and desired/commanded values.
  • Pitch Up/Vehicle Roll - measured attitude, alongside desired attitude and raw motor position (Pitch pos/Roll pos).
  • VBD pos/Buoyancy - VBD (buoyancy engine) position and the resulting modeled buoyancy.
  • sigma_t/Buoyancy Frequency - derived water-column properties along the track.
  • Heading (right axis, degrees true) - measured vs. desired heading, with the +/- error band.

This plot is the general-purpose starting point for diagnosing flight behavior; the more focused pitch/roll control plots are better for trim calibration specifically.
