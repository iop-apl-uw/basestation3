WETLabs Backscatter Plot Help

This plot shows all of the WETLabs puck's optical backscatter channels together vs. depth, dive and climb traces shown separately, preferring the basestation-adjusted value over the raw instrument value when both are available.

See the WETLabs fluorescence plot for the instrument's fluorescence channels (chlorophyll, CDOM, etc), which are plotted one channel per figure rather than combined.
