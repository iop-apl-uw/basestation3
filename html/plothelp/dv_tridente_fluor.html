Tridente Fluorescence Plot Help

This plot shows one Tridente fluorescence channel (e.g. chlorophyll, CDOM - named in the title) vs. depth, dive and climb traces shown separately. A separate plot is generated per fluorescence channel the instrument reports.

The annotation below the plot (when present) reports the instrument calibration comment and count of sample timeouts.

See the Tridente backscatter plot for the instrument's optical backscatter channels.
