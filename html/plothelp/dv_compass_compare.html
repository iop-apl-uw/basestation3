Compass Comparison Plot Help

This plot overlays the glider's primary ("truck") compass heading against a secondary compass, to help diagnose disagreement between them. The same layout is used regardless of which secondary compass is being compared - the title and trace names identify which one:

  • auxCompass - an auxiliary compass logger
  • auxB - a second auxiliary compass logger
  • ADCPCompass (from cp_* variables) - the compass built into a logdev-attached ADCP
  • ADCPCompass (from ad2cp_* variables) - the compass built into an AD2CP current profiler

Traces

For each of Depth, Pitch Up, Roll and Heading, both a truck and a secondary-compass trace are plotted so the two can be compared directly over time. Heading is on the right axis in degrees true; the others share the left axis.

The x-axis subtitle reports the RMS heading difference between the two compasses over the dive.

Large or growing disagreement usually indicates the secondary compass needs recalibration, or (for the truck compass) a magnetic interference issue near the compass location.
