VBD Efficiency Plot Help

This plot tracks VBD pump efficiency across the mission, vs. dive number. Only pump moves are shown (bleed moves are excluded), and points above the --max_vbd_effic threshold are dropped as outliers.

Each point is colored by the depth the pump move occurred at, so you can see whether efficiency depends on ambient pressure as well as on dive number. A steady downward trend in efficiency over the mission can indicate pump wear or an accumulating hydraulic issue - compare against the pump and bleed diagnostics plot for the underlying current/rate data.
