Pump and Bleed Diagnostics Plot Help

This plot tracks VBD (buoyancy engine) motor performance across the mission, vs. dive number - one point per pump or bleed move.

  • Pump Current (left axis, A) is motor current during pump moves (moves above the --max_vbd_pump_rate threshold only).
  • Pump Rate and Bleed Rate (right axis, AD/s) are VBD position change rate during pump and bleed moves respectively.

A falling pump rate or rising pump current over the mission can indicate increasing hydraulic resistance (e.g. a fouled or aging pump); compare against the VBD efficiency plot.
