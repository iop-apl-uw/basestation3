Surface Maneuver Depth and Angle Plot Help

This plot tracks the glider's surface communication maneuver over the course of the mission, vs. dive number.

  • Surface Maneuver Depth (left axis, m) is how deep the antenna/vehicle sat during the surface maneuver.
  • Surface Maneuver Angle (right axis, deg) is the pitch angle held during the surface maneuver.

Both axes are reversed so shallower/lower values plot toward the top. Trends or step changes here can indicate a change in surface trim, sea state, or antenna performance affecting how the glider manages its surface posture.
