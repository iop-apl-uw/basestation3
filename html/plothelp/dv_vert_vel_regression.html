Vertical Velocity Regression Fit Plot Help

This is the fit-quality diagnostic produced by the hydrodynamic regression (RegressVBD) used to derive buoyancy bias and drag coefficients (HD_A, HD_B) for the vertical velocity plot. Depending on the --regress_dives setting, the regression is fit either to just this dive or to a rolling window of recent dives, in which case this plot reflects the multi-dive fit rather than this dive alone.

Use it to judge whether the fitted hydrodynamic parameters are well-constrained by the data (a tight, consistent fit) or noisy/poorly determined (scattered residuals), before trusting the implied C_VBD/drag coefficient updates reported on the vertical velocity plot.
